Who We Are

A Ministry
Built on Faith

Lord's ARMY — Lord's Adventist Reign Ministry–Youth — is a group of Seventh-day Adventist young people committed to serving God and serving people. Founded in 2021 from a small group of aspiring Master Guides, we have grown into a vibrant Pathfinder Club reaching communities through camps, outreach, and evangelism.

Our journey has taken us from local investitures in Bukidnon to international recognition — including being featured in Guide Magazine. Members trained within Lord's ARMY have gone on to establish their own Pathfinder and Adventurer clubs across the region.

Our Purpose

Built to Serve

Everything we do flows from one conviction: that young people, empowered by faith, can change the world around them.

✝️
Serve God

Leading young people to Christ through ministry, worship, and outdoor adventure. Training leaders who will carry the gospel into their communities and beyond.

🤝
Serve People

Reaching remote communities through feeding programs, medical missions, book donations, and acts of kindness that demonstrate God's love in tangible, lasting ways.

📖
Share Hope

Distributing resources, sharing the Word, and bringing light to those who need it — through partnerships with BUGTAW Philippines, Red Cross Bukidnon, and others.

Recognition

From Bukidnon
to the World

What began as a small group of eight aspiring Master Guides in Libona, Bukidnon has grown into a ministry recognized on an international stage — a testimony to what God can do through committed young people.

In June 2025, Lord's ARMY was featured in Guide Magazine, a Pacific Press Seventh-day Adventist weekly periodical with global readership. The feature highlighted the club's unique journey — from campfires to community, from marching drills to medical missions.
