About Us

Who we are and what we stand for

Who Is Lord's ARMY?

Lord's ARMY Pathfinder Club is a group of Seventh-day Adventist young people committed to serving God and serving people. Led by a Master Guide, we combine outdoor adventure with Christian ministry.

We believe that young people can make a difference in their communities through camping, feeding programs, and sharing God's love through books and acts of service.

Our name "Lord's ARMY" reflects our commitment to be soldiers for Christ — serving faithfully, standing strong, and spreading hope wherever we go.
