Who We Are

A Ministry
Built on Faith

Lord's ARMY — Lord's Adventist Reign Ministry–Youth — is a group of Seventh-day Adventist young people committed to serving God and serving people. United by faith and a desire to make a lasting difference, we go where we are called.

Founded in 2021 from a small group of aspiring Master Guides, we have grown into a vibrant Pathfinder Club that reaches communities through camps, outreach, and evangelism. Our journey has taken us from local investitures to international recognition — including being featured in Guide Magazine.

Through outdoor adventures, community service, and leadership development, we equip young people to become confident, compassionate, and Christ-centered leaders. Members trained within our ministry have gone on to establish their own Pathfinder and Adventurer clubs across the region.

Our Journey

Milestones & Legacy

2021
August 13

First batch of aspiring Master Guides begins training under the Progressive Class, led by East Jordan Association Youth Department.

October 30

The "MAGIC 8" are officially invested by Pastor Tedy Baang at Crossing SDA Church, Libona, Bukidnon.

December 11

Second batch of Master Guides trained and invested.

2022
June 3

LORD'S ARMY Pathfinder Club formally established — officers inducted by Pastor Ebenezer Perez at Manolo Fortich SDA Church.

July 9

Twelve additional Master Guides invested by Pastor Virailio Teves Jr. in Lantapan, Bukidnon.

July 29

Constitution and By-Laws officially adopted, solidifying the club's identity and mission.

2023
Various Camps

Members participated in Pathfinder Camp/Fliesing, Pag-asa Youth Camp (as facilitators), and multiple district and provincial camps.

Outreach & Service

Expanded community outreach through medical assessments, gift-giving, and partnerships with BUGTAW Philippines and other organizations.

2024
International Recognition

Lord's ARMY featured in Guide Magazine — a Pacific Press SDA weekly periodical — marking a global milestone for the ministry.

Legacy Building

Members trained within Lord's ARMY became Club Directors of their own clubs: Lightfinders, Obsidian Armor, Herald, and Little Vanguard.

Meet Our Director

Master Guide  |  Nutrition-Dietitian

Arlie Joy Damiles is a dedicated Master Guide and a licensed Nutrition-Dietitian who brings a unique blend of professional expertise and spiritual passion to Lord's ARMY. With years of experience in youth ministry and community health, she leads our club with a holistic vision — caring for both the physical and spiritual well-being of every member.

Her background in nutrition and dietetics has shaped our outreach programs, emphasizing wellness as a key component of service. She is deeply committed to guiding young people toward Christ, while also equipping them with practical skills for leadership and healthy living.
