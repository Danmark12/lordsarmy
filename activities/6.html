Nauwanan ta Bawayan IP School Outreach

Crossing Mountains to Reach Indigenous Communities

2025 Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon 3 min read

"Command them to do good, to be rich in good deeds, and to be generous and willing to share."

— 1 Timothy 6:18

The Lord's ARMY Pathfinder Club and Bugtaw Philippines crossed mountains and canyons to reach Nauwanan ta Bawayan, an IP School in Brgy. Guilang-Guilang, Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon.

This remote indigenous community welcomed us with open arms, their smiles and warmth making the challenging journey worthwhile.

📚

Books & Bookshelves

Donated to support literacy

🍲

Feeding Program

Nourishing young learners

📖

Storytelling

Inspiring through stories

❤️

Health & Games

Free BP monitoring & interactive games

The outreach program included a feeding program, donation of books and bookshelves, storytelling sessions, free blood pressure monitoring for parents, and interactive games facilitated by Bugtaw PH Representative Ervin Andrew Yano and the Lord's ARMY Master Guides.

Activities like these not only nurture the learners' education but also allow them to enjoy recreational experiences that build community and confidence.

Nauwanan ta Bawayan IP School — Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon

All glory to God for pouring out blessings through our sincerest sponsors. The journey was long and challenging, but seeing the joy on the children's faces made every step worth it.

This outreach reminded us that education is a gift that can transform communities, and that every child deserves the opportunity to learn and grow.
