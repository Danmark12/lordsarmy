The Story

Global Youth Day 2025 marked a significant moment as the ministry went beyond the church, building a community transformed through education, awareness, and empowerment. As the Lord's ARMY, we serve with passion and purpose, giving hope to the overlooked and a voice to the unheard.

This Commitment Board is making a meaningful impact, reflecting our dedication to positive change. As we embrace the theme "A Community Transformed," it serves as a testament to our collective efforts in inspiring growth, service, and lasting transformation in the lives of those around us.

"Our ministry goes beyond the church, building a community transformed through education, awareness, and empowerment."

Lord's ARMY Pathfinder Club

Global Youth Day is a worldwide initiative that encourages young people to step out of their comfort zones and serve their communities. From street cleaning to feeding programs, from health awareness campaigns to educational initiatives, young people around the world made a difference.

The Commitment Board

The Commitment Board was a powerful symbol of the dedication and passion of the youth. Each commitment written on the board represented a promise to serve, to love, and to make a difference in the world. It was a visual reminder that transformation begins with a single act of courage and compassion.

As the Lord's ARMY, we serve with passion and purpose, giving hope to the overlooked and a voice to the unheard. Our commitment to service is not limited to one day — it is a lifelong calling.

"The Commitment Board is making a meaningful impact, reflecting our dedication to positive change."

Lord's ARMY Pathfinder Club
More Moments

Transformation Through Service

The theme "A Community Transformed" is not just a slogan — it is a reality that we are living out every day. Through education, awareness, and empowerment, we are building communities that are stronger, more compassionate, and more just.

We invite everyone to join us in this mission. Whether through a small act of kindness or a large-scale project, every effort counts. Together, we can create a world where hope is restored, voices are heard, and communities are transformed.
