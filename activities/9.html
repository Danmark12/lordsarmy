The Story

Physical fitness is often overlooked, yet it is a vital part of the tripod of education in Pathfindering. In support of a healthier lifestyle, the LA Runners are participating in the Run4Me: Run for Mother Earth event, alongside Pastor Teddy Ba-ang and his wife.

"Physical fitness is a vital part of the tripod of education in Pathfindering."

Lord's ARMY Pathfinder Club

May this physical journey continue to honor and celebrate our commitment to caring for our bodies through a healthy and active lifestyle. The event brought together runners from all walks of life, united by a shared passion for fitness and environmental stewardship.

Run Highlights
Run4Me: Run for Mother Earth 2025

The LA Runners demonstrated that fitness and environmental stewardship go hand in hand. By participating in this event, they showed that caring for our bodies is also caring for the world God has entrusted to us.
