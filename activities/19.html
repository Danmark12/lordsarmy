Dangcay Mission School has been a beacon of hope for the indigenous community in Kitaotao. Despite its remote location and limited resources, the school continues to provide education and opportunities for the children of the community.

This humanitarian mission brought together multiple organizations united by a common purpose: to serve the community and empower students through education and support. The collaboration demonstrated the power of unity in making a difference.

"Command them to do good, to be rich in good deeds, and to be generous and willing to share."

1 Timothy 6:18

Through this mission, we were able to provide essential resources and support to the students and community members of Dangcay Mission School. The impact of this outreach extends far beyond the material donations — it has touched hearts and inspired hope.

We extend our deepest gratitude to all our partners — Followers of God Society (FOGS), Bugtaw Philippines, Red Cross Bukidnon, and Diamond Civilian Employee Association — for their unwavering support in making this humanitarian mission a success.
