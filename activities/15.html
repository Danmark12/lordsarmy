The Story

We are truly inspired by the incredible individuals we worked with during the Pathfinder Survival Camp and Advanced Survival Camp. It's no surprise they are destined to become leaders in their respective churches.

We have gained so much from these amazing people. Kudos to all the youth of Division A Seventh-Day Adventist Youth Department at NEMM. The camp was a transformative experience that built character, strengthened faith, and developed leadership skills.
