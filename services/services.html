Serving with
Purpose

From high-altitude mountain camps to riverside cleanups, from classroom lectures to medical outreach — Lord's ARMY shows up wherever we are called to serve.

Our Services

Called to Serve

Lord's ARMY is not just a club — it is a ministry. Everything we do is rooted in the belief that faith without action is incomplete. We offer our time, skills, and hearts to serve God by serving people in practical, meaningful ways.

From facilitating youth camps and delivering lectures to conducting outreach in remote communities, our services are designed to equip the next generation of leaders while making a lasting difference in the lives of those we encounter.

"For even the Son of Man did not come to be served, but to serve, and to give his life as a ransom for many."

— Mark 10:45

Core Services

What We Do

Each service we offer is a deliberate act of faith — carried out by trained young people committed to excellence in ministry.

01
🏕️
Pathfinder Camp Facilitation
Leading Camps with Excellence

Lord's ARMY has been the trusted facilitator of the KSDAES Pathfinder and Adventurer Camp for four consecutive years. Our members plan, organize, and lead every aspect of camp — from devotional programs and investitures to outdoor skills and team-building activities. We bring structure, creativity, and a spirit of service to every camp we handle.

Camping Leadership Youth Annual
Full camp planning and program design
Spiritual and devotional program leadership
Outdoor skills and investiture ceremonies
4th consecutive year facilitating KSDAES
02
🎯
Pathfinder Class Lectures
Teaching the Next Generation

Our Master Guides serve as lecturers and instructors for Pathfinder Progressive Classes across the district. We cover core Pathfinder curriculum — from basic honor requirements and nature study to marching, drilling, and the CATS (Camping, Arts and Crafts, and others). Our goal is to produce well-rounded, disciplined, and spiritually grounded Pathfinders.

Education Lectures Master Guide Training
Progressive Class curriculum instruction
Marching, drilling, and sign language
CATS and outdoor survival skills
Honor requirements coaching
03
🪖
CMT Training Lectures
Citizen Military Training Support

Lord's ARMY members serve as resource persons and lecturers in Citizen Military Training (CMT) programs. We bring discipline, structure, and leadership formation experience to CMT sessions — instilling values of duty, honor, and service in young participants while supporting the educational institutions that host these programs.

CMT Military Training Discipline Schools
Resource speaking and lecture facilitation
Marching commands and formation drills
Leadership and values formation
Partnership with schools and institutions
04
⛺
Youth Camp Facilitation
Beyond Pathfinders — All Youth Welcome

Beyond the Pathfinder program, Lord's ARMY facilitates a wide range of youth camps — including Adventist Youth (AY) camps, survival camps, BSP and GSP encampments, and provincial gatherings. We have served as facilitators for the Pag-asa Youth Camp, NEMM Survival Camp, TSDACC Conquers' Camp, and more — bringing the same commitment to excellence to every event.

Youth Camps AY BSP / GSP Survival
AY Progressive Class facilitation
Survival camp training and leadership
BSP & GSP Encampment facilitation
Program design and event coordination
05
🤝
Community Outreach Programs
Taking the Ministry Beyond the Church

Our outreach programs bring tangible expressions of God's love to schools, IP communities, and underserved barangays across Bukidnon and beyond. These programs include feeding programs, book and school supply donations, storytelling sessions, free blood pressure monitoring, and prayer ministry. We have reached the Dangcay Mission School, Cayaga National High School, Nauwanan ta Bawayan IP School, and many more.

Outreach Feeding Books IP Communities
Feeding programs and meal distribution
Book and school supply donations
Free health monitoring and medical support
Storytelling, games, and spiritual ministry
06
🌿
Community Service & Cleanup
Caring for God's Creation

Stewardship of the environment is part of our calling. Lord's ARMY has conducted clean-up drives at Lagilay Falls, along rivers and drainage canals in Balulang, and in partnership with local communities to prevent flooding and promote environmental responsibility. We also install signage encouraging proper waste disposal — turning one-day events into lasting advocacy.

Environment Cleanup Advocacy Community
River, canal, and waterfall cleanup drives
Flood prevention community campaigns
Environmental signage installation
Partnership with LGUs and local orgs
07
⛪
Church & Ministry Volunteer Work
Serving the Body of Christ

We are committed to the local church and to the wider Adventist community. Lord's ARMY members volunteer for church programs, special Sabbath events, children's ministry, investiture ceremonies, and district gatherings. We also support World Adventurer Day, World Pathfinder Day, and Global Youth Day celebrations — bringing energy, creativity, and commitment to every occasion.

Church Volunteer Sabbath Ministry
Church event planning and volunteering
Children's ministry and Adventurer support
World Pathfinder Day & Global Youth Day
District and association gatherings
Why We Serve

The Heart
Behind the Work

✝️
Faith in Action

We believe that true faith is expressed through service. Every camp, lecture, and outreach we do is a declaration that our belief in God compels us to act — not just to worship inside a church, but to go out and make a difference.

🌱
Raising Leaders

Service develops character. Every time a Lord's ARMY member stands in front of a class, plans a camp, or leads a team into a remote community, they are growing into the leader God has called them to be.

🔗
Building Bridges

Our services connect people — churches to communities, youth to purpose, institutions to hope. We believe that young people positioned in the right place, at the right time, with willing hearts, can bridge gaps that others overlook.

Our Process

How We Prepare

Every service we offer goes through a deliberate process — because we believe that excellence honors God.

01
Prayer & Planning

Every service begins with prayer and careful planning. We identify needs, assign roles, set goals, and commit the entire effort to God before a single step is taken in the field.

02
Training & Preparation

Our members undergo relevant training before serving — whether reviewing curriculum for lectures, rehearsing camp programs, or preparing materials for community outreach.

03
Execution with Excellence

When the day of service arrives, we show up fully — committed, prepared, and present. We serve with discipline and warmth, representing both our club and our faith.

04
Reflection & Gratitude

After every service, we gather to reflect, give thanks, and learn. What worked? What can we improve? This culture of reflection keeps us growing with every mission.
